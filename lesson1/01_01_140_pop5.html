Dealing with Uncertainty

Policy efforts may be more uncertain and dynamic than programmatic efforts, and you generally do not have control over implementation, participants, and affected parties. Likewise, the affected parties may not have control over policies that are implemented. Although with programs, people often choose to participate or receive services, they may not have any choice as to whether to participate in a policy. In addition, there may be more variation in the timeline around policy efforts, whereas a programmatic effort is likely to have a known and defined timeframe, such as a specific funding period.
