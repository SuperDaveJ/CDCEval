What Is Policy?

Let’s begin by reviewing some basic information, starting with the definition of policy. Policy is one potentially effective way to improve the health of populations. At CDC, we define policy as a law, regulation, procedure, administrative action, incentive, or voluntary practice of governments and other institutions. Select each graphic to learn more.

Policy has been a useful tool in many public health achievements. You will learn more about those achievements in the next lesson.
