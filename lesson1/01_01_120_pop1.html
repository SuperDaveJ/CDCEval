Program

The term “program” is used to describe any organized public health action. Programs can include, among others

  • Direct service interventions
  • Community mobilization efforts
  • Research initiatives
  • Surveillance systems
  • Outbreak investigations
  • Laboratory diagnostics
  • Communication campaigns
  • Infrastructure building projects
  • Training and education services
  • Administrative systems

Source: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Framework for program evaluation in public health. MMWR 1999;48(No. RR-11):3.
