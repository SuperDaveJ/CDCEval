Why Evaluate Policy?

Uses of Policy Evaluation

Policy evaluation can have many uses.

  • Document and inform the policy development, adoption, and implementation process.
  • Determine policy effectiveness at improving health outcomes.
  • Gauge support for proposed policies.
  • Assess compliance within existing policies.
  • Contribute to thed evidence base.
  • Inform future policies and policy efforts.
  • Help identify results of policy efforts, including health outcomes.

One way to make explicit links between policies and health outcomes involves the use of logic models, which you will learn about later in this course.
