Step 3: Focusing the Evaluation Design

Considerations for Policy Evaluation

The design and focus of an evaluation will depend on which domain(s) of the policy process are being examined and the answers to the following questions:

  • Purpose: Why is the evaluation being conducted?
  • User: Who will use the information and what is their focus?
  • Use: How will the information gained from the evaluation be used?
When focusing the evaluation design, consider the feasibility and utility categories of evaluation standards.
