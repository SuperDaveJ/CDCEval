Outcomes

Outcomes are short-term, intermediate, and long-term changes in target audience behaviors, awareness of risk factors, attitudes, and knowledge. Long-term changes are often referred to as impacts. For the sake of this course, we’ll use the term “outcomes” to encompass impacts as well.

Examples of short-term/intermediate changes include:

  • Increased rates of physical activity
  • Attitudes toward healthy eating
  • Decrease in smoking
  • Use of seat belts
  • Change in community norms

Examples of long-term changes include:

  • Decrease in obesity
  • Decrease in healthcare-related infections
  • Decrease in injury rates
  • Decrease in morbidity
  • Decrease in mortality
  • Costs saved
