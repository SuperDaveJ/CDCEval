Step 4: Gathering Credible Evidence

Selecting Outcomes and Measures

Before you can understand what evidence you will need, you need to determine which outcomes you will measure—guided by the purpose, intended use, and users of the evaluation. The team should also identify the measures used to assess progress toward selected outcomes.

Try to balance subjective measures with additional objective or output measures, and keep in mind the context and content of the evaluation.
