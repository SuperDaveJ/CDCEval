Step 1: Engaging Stakeholders

Considerations for Policy Evaluation

When engaging stakeholders during a policy evaluation, you may encounter some unique challenges. Because multiple private and public sectors often participate in policy process, it can be challenging to come up with a complete list of potential stakeholders. Stakeholders from a variety of fields will provide unique and valuable perspectives on the policy evaluation.
