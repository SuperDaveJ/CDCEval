The Value of Policy Evaluation

Benefits of Public Health Policy Evaluation

How do we know that policy had anything to do with a reduction in tobacco use or the prevention of childhood lead poisoning? Policy evaluation and its related tools helped to demonstrate the public health impacts of policy.

Evaluation not only lets you know how effective a policy is; it can also inform the development of more effective policies which may translate into better public health outcomes. If policy is not evaluated, you may miss out on an opportunity to demonstrate how the policy was implemented and whether it was effective in improving public health.
